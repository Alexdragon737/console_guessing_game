This is a number guessing game that has about 4 levels of difficulty as of now:
-Baby level: guess a number between 1 and 10
-Intermediate: guess a number between 1 and 100
-Expert: guess a number between 1 and 1000
-Nostradamus or some crap: guess a number between 1 and the numeric limit of unsigned int(4294967295 to be more specific)

The game also features a hardcore mode, which integrates a lives system. The initial lives for each difficulty are:
-Baby level - 10 lives
-Intermediate - 7 lives
-Advanced - 4 lives
-Nostradamus or some crap - 1 life
